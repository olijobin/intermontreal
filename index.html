#interMontreal

Illustrate and celebrate the multiculturalism of Montreal.

1. Take a picture

Next time you stumble upon a scene that makes you feel like you're somewhere else in the world but that in fact you're in Montreal, take a picture.

2. Tag it (#interMontreal)

Publish your photo on Instagram and tell us where in the world you feel you are. Make sure that your description contains #interMontreal.

3. Get featured by us

We are constantly monitoring #interMontreal and we will republish our favorite photos.
